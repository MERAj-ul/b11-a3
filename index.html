FAQ

How often should I feed my cat?

Adult cats typically eat twice a day. Kittens may need more frequent meals. Always consult your vet for specific dietary advice.

Why is my cat kneading me?

Cats knead as a sign of comfort and contentment. It's an instinctive behavior they develop as kittens while nursing.

How can I stop my cat from scratching furniture?

Provide scratching posts and positive reinforcement when they use them. You can also use double-sided tape on furniture to discourage scratching.

What is the best way to introduce a new cat to my home?

Gradually introduce your new cat by providing a safe, quiet space first. Use scent swapping techniques and supervise initial interactions with other pets.
